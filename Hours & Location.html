Moody Towers Dining Hall

The 33,000 square feet Moody Towers Dining Commons operates 24/7 and provides delicious food using state-of-the-art equipment at ten different stations, with our professional chefs and experienced cooks that prepare all of the food right in front of you! Of course, it is all-you-care-to-eat so feel free to stop by all ten of the stations or just return to the same one as often as you'd like.
