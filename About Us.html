Our Story

Emily Bakes Cake was founded by Emily Boudreaux in 2003 in Houston, Texas.
Emily has a physical storefront where she sells her standard products: cakes, cupcakes, cookies, and pastries.
Her specialty is customizing and decorating European-style cakes for her customers.

Emily's Passion

Emily’s love of baking started when she was a young girl.
Her grandmother, would often include Emily whenever she was baking desserts for the family.
Mommie always said, “Everyone should have one special cake for their birthday or big event in their lives.”
Emily continues to honor her grandmother by upholding that tradition.

Later in life, Emily spent several years in Europe developing her skills as a baker.
She worked in a boulangerie (bakery) in Paris and spent a year in Salzburg, where she learned Bavarian and Austrian baking techniques.

Baking Philosophy

Emily Bakes Cakes prides itself on baking from scratch using family recipes combined with the European baking skills she acquired overseas.
This approach has led to robust sales at their physical store location near the Galleria.
